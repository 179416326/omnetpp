Image Export Plug In

This plug in provides an Eclipse wizard to export GEF diagrams to several formats (BMP, JPEG, etc.). Custom editors may easily contribute to make their diagrams exportable. The export formats BMP and JPEG are included. Plug ins that provide support for other formats may be found on the homepage.
